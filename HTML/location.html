Find Yodha Village (near Neem Ka Thana, Sikar, Rajasthan)

Yodha Village lies in the rural area surrounding Neem Ka Thana. Below are maps, directions and travel options to plan your visit.

Directions & Distances

From Neem Ka Thana (nearest town)
By road: ~10–30 km depending on exact village location (local roads). Travel time: 20–50 minutes.
From Sikar (district centre)
By road: ~40–60 km. Bus and private taxis available from Sikar.
From Jaipur (nearest major city / airport)
By road: ~110–140 km (2.5–3.5 hours). Jaipur International Airport is the closest major airport.
From Delhi
By road: ~250–300 km (5–6 hours depending on route). Train to Sikar + road transfer is an alternate option.

GPS / Coordinates (approx.)

Latitude: 27.7397° N, Longitude: 75.7865° E — use these in your GPS for better accuracy.

Transport Options

  • By Car / Taxi: Best option for direct access to the village; local drivers know village routes.
  • By Bus: State and private buses run to Neem Ka Thana; take a local taxi or shared vehicle to the village.
  • By Train: Nearest major station: Sikar Junction — then road transfer.
  • By Air: Jaipur International Airport — then hire a taxi or take a bus toward Sikar / Neem Ka Thana.

Tip: Confirm local road status during monsoon season; some rural roads may be slower.
