A genuine rural experience with community-first values

Yodha Village, near Neem Ka Thana (Sikar, Rajasthan), offers visitors and partners a blend of culture, safety and sustainable opportunities — ideal for cultural visits, agro‑tourism, learning traditional crafts and responsible collaboration.

Contact the Village Office

Top reasons to choose Yodha Village

  • Authentic Culture: Participate in local festivals, food and folk music.
  • Community Hospitality: Family‑friendly stays and guided local experiences.
  • Agro & Craft Learning: Hands‑on farming, animal husbandry and artisan workshops.
  • Safe & Accessible: Close to Neem Ka Thana with road links to Sikar and Jaipur.
  • Support Local Economy: Your visit helps artisans and farmers directly.

Programs & Opportunities

We support short cultural stays, school field trips, craft workshops, farm visits and small group tours. Custom programs can be arranged via the village panchayat.

Who should visit?

Students, cultural travelers, researchers, NGOs and small tour groups interested in sustainable rural development.

Quick Info & Location

Nearest town: Neem Ka Thana (road access)

Nearest railway: Sikar Junction (~40–60 km)

Nearest airport: Jaipur (~120 km)
